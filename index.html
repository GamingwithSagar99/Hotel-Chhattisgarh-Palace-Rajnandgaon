Break Journey

A Refreshing Oasis
On Your Journey

Conveniently located on the Rajnandgaon-Nagpur NH-53 corridor, Hotel Chhattisgarh Palace is the ideal stop for highway travelers seeking comfort, cleanliness, and convenience. Our hygienic AC rooms offer the perfect place to rest and recharge during long journeys.

With ample free parking, a family-friendly environment, and a 24/7 Tata Power EV charging station on-site, Chhattisgarh Palace is a complete break-journey destination for tired travelers and families. Enjoy our spacious playground, perfect for kids and family relaxation.
